Wintertime… In the hearth, a fire roars, throwing out its light and casting shadows. The wood crackles as flames slowly consume the logs, releasing their dense, smoky scent.

Burning time : 60 hours

Olfactory accident

- When using the candle for the first time, leave it to burn for 2 or 3 hours, until all the wax has become liquid on the surface.

- Regularly cut the wick with the wick trimmer (ideal length 3-5 mm).

- Be sure to centre the wick in the wax after each use for consistent consumption of the wax.

Here are some tips that will help you make the most out of your candle in the best conditions.
